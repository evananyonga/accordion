We provide skills-based training on construction contracts and claims to empower our graduates with the knowledge, skills, and competence to work as construction contract and claims professionals.

We improve professional standards within the specialist field of construction law and ensure that projects are run professionally, on-time and in accordance with the provisions of the contract.

Through our training we hope to reduce the number of construction claims and ensure that in cases where claims do arise, they are resolved quickly and professionally by the people charged with that responsibility.
